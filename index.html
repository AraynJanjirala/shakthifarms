About Shakti Farms

A local hydroponic initiative to bring chemical‑free freshness to Hyderabad households.

What is Hydroponics?

Hydroponics is soil‑less cultivation where plants grow in water enriched with precise nutrients. This allows clean, efficient, and faster growth with far less water compared to soil farming.

Why It Matters

  • Zero soil contaminants and no harmful pesticides
  • Year‑round, consistent harvests
  • Up to 90% less water usage
  • Traceable, transparent farm‑to‑home
